MSD GROUP, MSD is one of the Top 10 Engineering Colleges in Mumbai, Maharastra which was set up under the aegis of Mks Educational and Social Trust in 2001. The college offers a multitude of under graduate and post graduate degree courses in different branches of engineering and management. Proximity of regions premier educational and research institutes like National Institute of Technology, Maharastra; Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay; have resulted in vast pool of intellectual capital. Today, its infrastructure includes airy classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, safe and secure hostels for boys and girls, Multigym, Greenfield playgrounds to name a few. Many high profile Ministers, dignitaries and Academicians of national repute have visited and praised the college campus and its infrastructure.
